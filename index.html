Welcome to Quire


A free online text notepad. What can you do? Create different notepages and scribble down your notes. Save them and it will be stored in Quire. Come back anytime you would like and edit, delete, structure your content. All notes are dated automatically from when you edit in them. You have an really easy overview of when you created something. Mark you note with a star to bookmark it. So you can more easily navigate to your marked page.
